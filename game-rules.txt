Rules of the game
1. The game is played in a 3x3 grid
2. Players take turns putting their marks in empty squares.
3. The first player to get 3 of their marks in a row (vertically, horizontally or diagonally) is the winner.
4. When all 9 squares are full, the game is over.
5. If no player has 3 marks in a row, the game ends in a tie.
